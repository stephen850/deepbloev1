About

WHERE
MUSIC
MEETS BUSINESS.

A global electronic music talent, programming
and industry platform. Connecting artists, promoters,
festivals, clubs and brands, worldwide.

Deepbloe is a full-service electronic music platform, we represent artists, buy and program talent for festivals and clubs, build industry showcases and create strategic brand partnerships. All under one roof, worldwide.

We're not a traditional booking agency. We are a boutique partner built for the long game, with deep industry expertise, a global network and the personal attention that serious careers demand.

From Tomorrowland to S2O Thailand, from intimate club residencies to landmark festival stages, we position our artists, clients and partners where they belong.

What we do

SERVICES

01 TALENT REPRESENTATION Full-service artist representation built on long-term vision. We handle bookings, negotiations, routing and career strategy — so artists can focus on the music. Every artist we represent is a partnership, not a transaction.
↗
02 STRATEGIC PARTNERSHIPS We connect brands to music culture in ways that feel genuine. Drawing on years of experience on both sides of the table, we identify the right fit, structure the deal and manage the relationship — from initial concept to final delivery.
↗
03 INDUSTRY SHOWCASES We create and produce our own showcase events at ADE, WMC and other key industry moments. These are not just shows — they are strategic platforms that generate visibility, press and business opportunities for the artists and partners involved.
↗
04 TALENT BUYING & PROGRAMMING We programme stages, festivals, club nights, beach clubs, luxury venues and brand experiences — globally. From full festival line-up curation to individual artist placement, we advise on what works, for whom, and why. Our programming work is built on direct market knowledge and long-term promoter relationships across Europe, Latin America and MENA.
↗
05 MANAGEMENT & CONSULTANCY Beyond bookings, we advise artists and brands on strategy, positioning and growth. Our consultancy work draws on direct experience across talent, touring, brand partnerships and market development. Whether you need a sounding board or a full strategic overhaul, we bring honest, informed perspective — with no agenda other than your success.
